What is NAVKRITI ?

The term Navkriti is a Sanskrit word. In sanskrit "Nava" means "new" and "kriti" means "Creation". so "navakriti" could be translated to mean "new Creation". Navkriti is annual technical fest of chaibasa engineering college.
